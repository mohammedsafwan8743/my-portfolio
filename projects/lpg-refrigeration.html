Fabrication of LPG Refrigeration System

LPG-powered refrigeration for off-grid food preservation and rural sustainability.

  • A mechanical engineering project focused on designing and fabricating a refrigeration system powered by LPG, eliminating the need for electricity.
  • Utilized liquefied petroleum gas as a dual-purpose energy source for both cooking and cooling applications.
  • Developed a sustainable and cost-effective cooling solution suitable for rural and off-grid areas.
  • Optimized the design for energy efficiency and environmental sustainability.
  • Demonstrated the potential of LPG-based thermal systems as an alternative to conventional refrigeration methods.
